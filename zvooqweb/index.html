Zvooq web

Zvooq is now one of the leading licensed music services in Russia and CIS.
In 2011 it was launched as a web service for music access, discovery and collection.


I was involved in the launch and further iterations of web product during my time at Zvooq. My role included drafting and discussing product roadmaps, balancing user needs and business goals, and advocating for better UX. I wrote design documents, created prototypes and mockups, designed myself and and coordinated a team of two designers.

Here are some features that I was working on:


Player queue

Designing player queue userflows and mockups, we were facing questions like this: "A user has added an album to the queue and then wants to remove few tracks from that album from the queue. Will that be possible to do and what remains in the queue afterwards? Will that still be an album or a tracklist?"


Social discovery / friend feed

Social music discovery was a strong side of Zvooq web service. We used grid view to utilize maximum of the screen space.

User profile

We went through several iterations of user profile editor.

Landing page framework

Queue is a feature that allows users to put music for future listening. Seems simple, until We have gone through several design concepts.
